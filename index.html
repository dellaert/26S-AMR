Course Description

Mobile robots—ground vehicles, legged robots, underwater vehicles, drones, and space robots—are increasingly prevalent across diverse domains. This course provides a comprehensive introduction to the mathematical foundations of robot mobility, emphasizing optimization, estimation, and control.
